Space Facts

Earth's Moon

Overview

The Moon is Earth's only natural satellite and the fifth largest moon in the Solar System. It is about 384,400 km from Earth and has a diameter of 3,474 km. The Moon's gravitational influence is the main driver of Earth's tides.

Key Facts

  • Diameter: 3,474 km
  • Distance from Earth: ~384,400 km
  • Orbital period: 27.3 days
  • Surface gravity: 0.165 g (16.5% of Earth's)
  • Surface temperature: -173°C to 127°C

Did You Know?

  • The Moon always shows the same face to Earth (synchronous rotation).
  • It is the only celestial body beyond Earth visited by humans.
  • The Moon has a very thin atmosphere called an exosphere.
