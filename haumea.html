Haumea

Overview

Haumea is a dwarf planet located in the Kuiper Belt. It is notable for its elongated, ellipsoid shape, rapid rotation, and the presence of a ring system. Haumea has two known moons, Namaka and Hi'iaka.

Key Facts

  • Diameter: ~1,632 × 1,014 × 812 km (ellipsoid)
  • Distance from Sun: ~6.5 billion km (43.1 AU)
  • Orbital period: 284 Earth years
  • Rotation period: ~3.9 hours (fastest of any known equilibrium body in the Solar System)
  • Moons: 2 (Namaka and Hi'iaka)
  • Has a ring system

Did You Know?

  • Haumea's rapid rotation causes its elongated shape.
  • It is named after the Hawaiian goddess of childbirth and fertility.
  • Haumea's ring was discovered in 2017.
