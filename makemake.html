Makemake

Overview

Makemake is a dwarf planet in the Kuiper Belt and is one of the largest known objects in that region. It has a reddish color and a very low temperature. Makemake has one known moon, discovered in 2016.

Key Facts

  • Diameter: ~1,430 km
  • Distance from Sun: ~6.85 billion km (45.8 AU)
  • Orbital period: 305 Earth years

Did You Know?

  • Makemake is named after a deity from the mythology of Easter Island.
  • It is one of the brightest objects in the Kuiper Belt.
  • Makemake's moon is nicknamed MK2.
