Earth

Overview

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only astronomical object known to harbor life. Its surface is 71% water, and it has a protective atmosphere that supports a diverse range of climates and ecosystems.

Key Facts

  • Diameter: 12,742 km
  • Distance from Sun: ~149.6 million km (1 AU)
  • Orbital period: 365.25 days
  • Atmosphere: 78% nitrogen, 21% oxygen, 1% other gases
  • Surface gravity: 9.8 m/sÂ²
  • One natural satellite: the Moon

Did You Know?

  • Earth's magnetic field protects it from solar and cosmic radiation.
  • Earth is the densest planet in the Solar System.
  • It is the only planet not named after a mythological figure.
