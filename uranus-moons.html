Uranus' Moons

Overview

Uranus has at least 27 known moons, named mostly after characters from the works of William Shakespeare and Alexander Pope. The five largest moons are Miranda, Ariel, Umbriel, Titania, and Oberon, each with unique geological features.

Key Facts

  • Largest moons: Miranda, Ariel, Umbriel, Titania, Oberon
  • Miranda has some of the most extreme cliffs and valleys in the Solar System
  • Moons are mostly composed of ice and rock
  • Discovered between 1787 and 1948
  • Many moons have been visited by Voyager 2

Did You Know?

  • Miranda's Verona Rupes is the tallest known cliff in the Solar System (up to 20 km high).
  • Uranus' moons are named after literary characters, not mythological figures.
  • Voyager 2 is the only spacecraft to have visited Uranus and its moons.
